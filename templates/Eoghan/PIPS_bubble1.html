Precision in Psychiatry Study

Thank you for expressing interest in our study! We are scientists at Trinity College Dublin who work on trying to figure out how to improve treatments in mental health.

Participation can be completed from your home!

It solely requires answering questions about:
  1. Your mental health treatment;
  2. Your symptoms;
  3. Aspects of your life at present;
  4. Aspects of your life in the past;
... and playing some games for us.
